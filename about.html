About Us

Amelia Street Dental, your trusted QIP Accredited Dental Practice dedicated to providing exceptional oral health care. Our team consists of skilled Dentists, Hygienists, Oral Health Therapists, and Nurses, all committed to delivering top-notch services that meet the highest standards in the industry.
At Amelia Street Dental, we prioritize your dental health and well-being above all else. With our expertise and state-of-the-art facilities, we offer a comprehensive range of dental services tailored to suit your individual needs. Whether you require routine check-ups, preventive care, cosmetic treatments, or restorative procedures, rest assured that you're in capable hands.
We understand the importance of staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in dental technology and techniques. That's why our team continuously undergoes training to ensure we provide you with the most effective and innovative treatments available.
As a QIP Accredited Dental Practice, we adhere to stringent quality standards to guarantee the safety and satisfaction of our patients. Your comfort and peace of mind are our top priorities, and we strive to create a welcoming and stress-free environment for every visit.
Experience exceptional dental care with a personal touch at Amelia Street Dental. Contact us today to schedule your appointment and take the first step towards achieving a healthy and beautiful smile.
