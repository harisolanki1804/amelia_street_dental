What to expect during your Exam and Clean appointment?

During your routine exam and clean, we begin by assessing your dental health, noting any decay, fillings, or missing teeth. We check the condition of existing fillings and assess gum health, removing plaque and calculus build-up. X-rays help us detect hidden issues. Our experienced team ensures your comfort throughout the visit. We polish your teeth for a sparkling finish and apply fluoride gel for prevention. Finally, we conduct a thorough visual examination, share intraoral pictures, and provide tips for preventing tooth decay and gum disease.
